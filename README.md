# LEST: Low Entropy Systems Theory

**A simple way to understand why systems work, slow down, or break down**

---

## Core Idea

> **Things that keep moving tend to work.  
Things that build up tend to break.**

---

## The Governing Dynamic

> **Entropy increases by default.  
Coherence must be maintained.**

---

## What This Means

All systems:

- take in inputs  
- process energy or information  
- produce outputs  
- degrade if flow stops  

---

## The Simple Model

> **Wiggle = healthy  
Drag = resistance  
Stuck = accumulation**

---

## Why It Matters

> **Clarity and flow are competitive advantages in complex environments**

---

## Real-World Example: Kanban

> **Where flow stops, accumulation begins**
