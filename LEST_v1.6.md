## 1. Abstract

LEST (Low Entropy Systems Theory) defines a governing dynamic applicable to active systems:

> **Entropy increases by default.  
Coherence must be maintained.**

Active systems process energy and matter to sustain structure and function. As energy is transformed, entropy increases. System performance depends on how effectively higher-entropy outputs are transferred out of the system.

LEST describes system behavior.  
HiCo (High Coherence) represents a practical path for improving outcomes within that behavior.
